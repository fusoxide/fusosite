My music


I have been creating a variety of different genres of music since 2012. Here are some of my works.

Solo work


I have made many tracks, and can create pretty much any genre with the right motivation. Below is fusoxide's Works Vol. 1, an album consisting of tracks I've created over the years, encompassing genres such as chiptune, jazz, house, synthpop and plunderphonics. You should consider downloading or buying it if you like what you hear!



I also created an album named fusoxide's Ringtones Vol. 1, which has 24+ ringtones I composed in a variety of genres throughout 2021, and some were based on material dating back to 2015-2020 as well. If you've both been looking for a new ringtone and enjoy my music, you've come to the right place. :) There's also three bonus remixes, one is my own and the other two were created by friends.



I have more projects in the works. Stay tuned!
